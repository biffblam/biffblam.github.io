UPDATE: I neglected to include the sale of the spec script "Deathgames" by Michael Hultquist & Robert Martinez. The movie has since been renamed The Killing Game and is in post-production. It stars Samuel L. Jackson and James Remar. It's especially odd I failed to include this because Mike has taken several of my screenwriting courses. I did, however, mention the movie here and here.

So that brings the total to 55 spec script sales in 2010.
