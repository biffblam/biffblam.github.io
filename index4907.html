Firefox 4 makes it trickier to easily add a site's RSS feed.

Previously it automatically displayed an RSS icon at right in the address bar.  Now you need to click the bookmarks icon at far right of the top bar then choose 'Subscribe to this page'.

Click for RSS feed (http://10ptt.com/smf/index.php?type=rss;action=.xml;limit=10).

Or add it manually:

http://10ptt.com/smf/index.php?type=rss;action=.xml;limit=10

The feed shows public topics/posts only.
