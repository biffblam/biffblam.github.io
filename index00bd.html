Spec Script Sale: “Blood Drive”
« on: February 15, 2013, 05:03 PM »
Sentinel Pictures acquires action thriller spec script “Blood Drive” from writer Christian Parkes. From Variety:

Story centers on an everyday guy with the rarest of blood types who is suddenly and unexpectedly forced to deliver his very own, freshly stolen kidney to an infamous criminal whose life depends on it.

Ticking clock = pressure = conflict = drama. With a twist: Delivering his own kidney.

Parkes is repped by Zero Gravity Management.

By my count, this is the 4th spec script sale in 2013.

Last year at this time, there were 10 spec sales.
