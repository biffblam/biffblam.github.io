Title: Spec Script Sale: "Tipping Point"
Post by: Tom & Jerry on May 23, 2011, 02:29 AM
Relativity Media buys action-thriller spec script "Tipping Point" from writer Todd Stein. Via It's On The Grid:
Based on his original short story, set a blink into the future, New York cop Solomon is part of an elite police division hunting those who break the Zero Population Act of2027. A routine stop alters Solomon’s life forever when he uncovers the woman he once loved had an illegal child… his son. Now a criminal himself, Solomon must protect his family from the very task force he works for as he races thru New York to find a way out.
Script is based on a short story Stein wrote and was posted on the website Popcorn Fiction. The site is hosted by screenwriter-novelist Derek Haas, so here is a case where one screenwriter helped create some heat for another screenwriter. Which is to say congrats to Mr. Stein and good on you, Mr. Haas!

Stein is repped by WME and Industry Entertainment Partners.

By my count, this is the 37th spec script sale to sell in 2011.

The 37th spec didn't sell in 2010 until September 23rd.

The spec market is now more than 4 months ahead of last year's sale pace.
