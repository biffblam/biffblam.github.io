Hi, DZ.

It's a one-horse town round these parts, with me being the sole 10PTTer so far.  That's okay.  But it does mean those who post their ten pages aren't likely to get satisfaction in a timely manner.  Me choosing pages to 10PTT and scheduling the work is about as random as random gets.

I can't guarantee I'll 10PTT your pages, but thank you for sending them.  That goes out not just to DZ but to everyone who has or will ask me to 10PTT their stuff.

Anyone who wants to take a shot at doing a 10PTT can email or pm DZ directly to get a copy of his pages.

Oh, and just because I neglect to 10PTT somebody's pages, don't take that as negative judgement against the work.  Being more of a mechanic than a creator, if I could I'd happily spend all day doing 10PTTs to get that delicious flush of contentment that comes from performance tuning a paragraph, not unlike that loop sequence from BRAINSTORM.  Yeah, that one.  So thank heavens I need to pay for more than electricity and an internet connection on a daily basis.
