Title: First 10PTT to tally 1000 views
Post by: Pitchpatch on November 01, 2012, 10:35 AM
Woo-hoo!  23 MINUTES (http://10ptt.com/smf/index.php/topic,293.0.html) by Trevor Mayes just became the first 10PTT to cross the 1K-views threshold -- a small but satisfying milestone.

Over the past month it streaked ahead of THE BUNKER (http://10ptt.com/smf/index.php/topic,272.0.html) and ATTACK THE BLOCK (http://10ptt.com/smf/index.php/topic,221.0.html).  Those two were the site heavyweights until 23 MINUTES came along.

I had enormous fun with Trevor's pages, his passion for writing, his dedication to the craft, and his infectiously positive outlook.  Not hard to imagine me adding a coda to that topic in a year or two announcing Trevor bagged his first big sale.
